Taxes & National Revenue

Manage your civil duties comfortably online.

Department of National Revenue (DNR)

The Department of National Revenue is committed to fair, transparent, and efficient tax collection to fund the structural and social development of San Meridian.

  • File personal income tax returns.
  • Register business entities and VAT.
  • Pay municipal property taxes.

Important Dates

  • Personal Income Tax Filing April 30
  • Corporate Tax Q1 Payment May 15
  • Property Tax Assessment June 01

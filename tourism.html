Top Destinations

Experience the vibrant culture and breathtaking landscapes of our beloved nation.

Dos Cervezas

A major city known for its rapid urban expansion. Explore the bustling streets, experience the vibrant culture of the favelas, and witness the striking contrast between historical roots and modern development.

Los Islas del Marmalingas

A beautiful, untouched archipelago resort area. Relax in luxurious overwater bungalows, swim in crystal-clear turquoise waters, and enjoy the ultimate tropical paradise getaway.

Pogat Cordillera

A rugged and towering mountain range, perfect for adventurous spirits. Scale massive sheer rock faces on thrilling klettersteig via ferrata routes and hike through deep, dramatic ravines.

Travel Planning & Visas

San Meridian welcomes tourists from across the globe. Citizens of over 80 countries enjoy visa-free travel for up to 90 days.

Check our digital portal to see if your passport qualifies for visa-free entry. E-Visas are available for all other nationalities and are typically processed in 48 hours.

The official currency is the Meridian Peso (SM$). Credit cards are widely accepted in urban areas. Check our latest health and safety advisories before traveling.

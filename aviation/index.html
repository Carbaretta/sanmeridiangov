Pilot Licensing

Thinking of taking to the skies? The SMAA provides comprehensive certification services for aspiring and professional pilots. Whether you're interested in a Private Pilot License (PPL) or pursuing a career in commercial aviation, our standards ensure you are fully prepared.

Private Pilot License (PPL)
  • Min Age: 17 years old
  • Class 2 Medical Certificate required
  • 45 hours minimum flight time
  • Written & Practical examinations
PPL Checklist
Commercial Pilot (CPL)
  • Min Age: 18 years old
  • Class 1 Medical Certificate required
  • 200 hours minimum flight time
  • Advanced navigation & IR rating

Aircraft Registration

All aircraft operating within the sovereign airspace of San Meridian must be registered with the SMAA and display a valid SM- prefixed tail number. Registration ensures your vehicle meets national airworthiness standards.

New Registration System: You can now manage your aircraft registration and renewal entirely online through the National Aviation Registry portal.
